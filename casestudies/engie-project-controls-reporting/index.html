Overview

BaseOne automated project controls reporting for a global energy company on the Hinckley Point C nuclear power station.

The Customer

Active in the UK energy market for over 20 years, Engie is a French multinational utility company, headquartered in France. Engie operates in the fields of energy transition, electricity generation and distribution, natural gas, nuclear, renewable energy, and petroleum.

It is one of the few players in the sector to develop expert skills in both upstream (engineering, purchasing, operation, maintenance) and downstream (waste management, dismantling) activities.

Engie supplies electricity in twenty-seven countries in Europe and forty-eight countries worldwide.

 

The Challenge

Engie had scope to manufacture thousands of HVAC systems for the Hinkley Point C site. The scope included the design, manufacture, and delivery of each item, plus logistics for transport between manufacture, testing and delivery.

Engie needed detailed scheduling in Primavera P6 and regular reporting to support the daily operations.

Due to the amount of schedule, documentation, quality and resource management, there was lots of siloed departmental data generated. This took a large amount of time to gather and report for the team, who spent a large portion of their time, creating, and updating reports.

A better solution was needed to help people report and give time back to the team to focus on their work.

 

Our Solution

BaseOne embedded two people into the Engie team. They created a suite of Power BI reports and simple ETL process that gathered data from many sources, including Primavera P6 XERs for baseline and actual projects containing 100k+ activities.

A single data model was developed that combined all data across documentation, planning, cost, and quality departments.

We created a of suite of Power BI reports that provided insights and reporting for senior management, managers and team members that tracked all activity, performance, documentation, quality, testing and logistics.

We enabled Agile delivery and trained team members, helping them move into bi-weekly delivery cadences with a focus on continuous improvement.
